UNCLE VANYA - Anton Checkov

VOITSKI takes a bottle from the drawer of the table and hands it to ASTROFF.

VOITSKI.

There it is!

[To SONIA]

And now, we must get to work at once; we must do something, or else I shall not be able to endure it.

SONIA.

Yes, yes, to work! As soon as we have seen them off we shall go to work.

[She nervously straightens out the papers on the table]

Everything is in a muddle!

ASTROFF.

[Putting the bottle in his case, which he straps together]

Now I can be off.

HELENA.

comes in.

HELENA.

Are you here, Ivan? We are starting in a moment. Go to Alexander, he wants to speak to you.

SONIA.

Go, Uncle Vanya.

[She takes VOITSKI 'S arm]

Come, you and papa must make peace; that is absolutely necessary.

HELENA.

I am going away.

[She gives ASTROFF her hand]

Good-bye.
